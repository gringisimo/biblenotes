TREASURES FROM GOD’S WORD | PSALMS 135-141

We Are Wonderfully Made

David meditated on the evidence of God’s good qualities reflected in His creation. He confidently used his life to serve Jehovah.

When David thought deeply about creation, he was moved to praise Jehovah:

139:14

  • “I praise you because in an awe-inspiring way I am wonderfully made”

139:15

  • “My bones were not hidden from you when I was made in secret, when I was woven in the depths of the earth”

139:16

  • “Your eyes even saw me as an embryo; all its parts were written in your book”

^ (Ps. 139:14) I praise you because in an awe-inspiring way I am wonderfully made. Your works are wonderful, I know* this very well.

Or “My soul knows.”

^ (Ps. 139:15) My bones were not hidden from you When I was made in secret, When I was woven in the depths of the earth.

^ (Ps. 139:16) Your eyes even saw me as an embryo; All its parts were written in your book Regarding the days when they were formed, Before any of them existed.
